Congratulations on surviving another Do West Fest!
Time to tally the chits.

Instructions:

  1. Add each item type you need to count, and the price. (Tall Cans, Dark and Stormy, Mixed Drink, Mixed Drink Double, Soda, Non-Alc Beer, etc.)
  2. Enter your first chit. The total of that chit will be shown on the right.
  3. Write that down!
  4. Click Next chit — the receipt total will clear, and be added to the overall total.
  5. Continue inputting all the chits the same way.
  6. The total amount of all chits entered will continue to be tracked on the side.
  7. When you've entered your last chit for the day, hit Next chit and (IMPORTANT!) write down the Running Total for that batch/day.
  8. Click Clear total when you're ready to start tallying the next batch.

Notes:

  • I recommend messing around with it for a minute before getting into properly tallying. Add items, remove items, hit the buttons, see what it does.
  • Made a mistake and want to remove the last chit from the Running Total? Hit Remove last chit.
  • If you want to calculate cash sales separate from digital sales: As you go, don't include the receipts that say they were paid in cash (assuming the servers marked that down.) Put them aside. Once you get through all the chits for the day, you have your pile of cash paid chits. You write down your digital sales total, hit Clear total, and then tally up those cash chits you put aside. TL;DR: Do all the day's digital sales chits, write it down, clear the total, and then do the day's cash sales, write it down, clear the total.
